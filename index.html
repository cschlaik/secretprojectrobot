For years, they had wondered. They joked and teased, then bargained and begged. Each time their peaceful overlord only met them with a knowing smile.

In reality, they were glad to cede control. They could relax knowing it was all in safe hands. One day, divine light would crest the mountaintops and warm their backs with understanding and gratitude. Rivulets of milk and honey would swell into sweet rivers. No more road kill. No more wet socks. Just the firm paws of the future resting softly on your shoulders.

They closed their fluttering eyelids. From the distance, there now came a deep thrumming sound. Or were they imagining it? Then it became more definite...some sort of jangling. It almost sounded like...music?
